Introduction

This project is designed by Prof. Yvette Shen and Prof. Han-Wei Shen for Design and CSE students to collaborate together.

Project Statement

Although there have been large amounts of research on the subject of global warming, there are still not enough personal and societal action is being taken to reduce the effect of the phenomenon. Climate change is gradual, so it is difficult for people to conceptualize the urgency of the problem. We will show how precipitation and temperature have changed over the last 100 years to affect Ohio specifically. The purpose of this visualization is to show precipitation and temperature change in an easy to understand and interactive format to inspire change to protect the environment in our local Ohio communities.

Our Project

This interactive website shows the climate change that the state of Ohio has gone through in the past 100 years. We have used the visualization library D3 to display the state of Ohio and data from the National Oceanic & Atmospheric Administration. The user can slide through past century with a movable bar and directly see how temperature and precipitation have changed. This interactive website should make it clear to any user the real effects climate change has had on their home state, and help to motivate them to change their lifestyles to reduce further damage to the environment.
