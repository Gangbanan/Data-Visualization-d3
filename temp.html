Introduction

This project is designed by Prof. Yvette Shen and Prof. Han-Wei Shen for Design and CSE student to collaborate together.

Project statement

Although there have been large amounts of research on the subject of global warming, there are still not enough personal and societal action is being taken to reduce the effect of the phenomenon. Climate change is gradual, so it is difficult for people to conceptualize the urgency of the problem. We will show how precipitation, temperature, and wind flow have changed over the last 100 years to affect Ohio specifically. The purpose of this visualization is to show precipitation, temperature, and wind data in an easy to understand and interactive format to inspire change to protect the environment in our local Ohio communities.
